01 · DSP Engine & Equalizer

One switch, then dial in the sound

The big DSP ON switch turns the whole processing chain on or off — everything below reacts live. Under Audio Controls you get three quick shapers: Bass Boost for weight, Loudness for presence at low volume, and Virtual Surround to widen the stage.

The heart of it is a true 10-band equalizer, from 32 Hz all the way to 16 kHz, each band swinging between −15 and +15 dB. A live response curve shows exactly what you're doing to the sound, and one-tap presets like Flat, Rock and Jazz get you started fast.

  • Master DSP on/off with clear "DSP Active" status
  • 10 bands · 32 Hz → 16 kHz · ±15 dB each
  • Live EQ response curve and quick presets

02 · Smart DSP & Profiles

Smart processing, saved for every mood

Smart DSP does the clever work automatically: Dynamic Loudness keeps the volume even between quiet and loud scenes, Dialogue Enhancer lifts voices out of a busy mix so you never reach for subtitles, and the Anti-Clipping Limiter protects your speakers from harsh peaks.

Found a setup you love? Save and Load full profiles, or drop it into a ready slot — Movie, Music, Voice or Night — and switch between them in a single tap. On Android TV every control is fully D-pad friendly, so it all works from the couch with the remote.
