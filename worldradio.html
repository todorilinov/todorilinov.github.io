Inside the app
01 · Home

Everything you listen to, on one screen

The home screen puts your whole radio life in front of you. Recently Played brings back the stations you just heard, while 🔥 Trending Worldwide shows the Top 10 stations people are listening to right now across the globe.

One-tap cards jump straight to your Favorites and the Alarm, and the mini player at the bottom keeps the current station — and the song playing on it — always in reach.

  • Reorder button (top right) — arrange your country list exactly how you want it
  • Tap & hold a country you've added to remove it from the list — the same works for stations you've added
  • Search bar to instantly find any country
02 · Browse by Country

Build your own map of the radio world

Browse stations country by country — each entry shows the flag and how many stations are inside. Brazil, the Netherlands, Portugal, Bosnia… and when a country you want isn't there yet, just hit ＋ Add Country and it joins your list.

The list is yours to control: reorder countries so your favorites sit on top, and remove any country you've added with a simple tap & hold. Radio keeps playing in the mini player the whole time you browse.

Country flags Station counts ＋ Add Country Custom order
02b · Stations

Pick a station, tap the heart

Open any country and you get a clean list of its stations — each with its own logo, name and genre, from BBC Radio 1 and BBC Radio 6 Music to Kiss FM UK and BBC World Service. Tap the ❤️ on any station to save it straight to your Favorites.

Just like the country list, this one is yours to arrange: use the reorder button in the top corner to sort your stations, and tap & hold any station you've added yourself to remove it.

Genre labels One-tap favorites Reorder stations Station logos
03 · Now Playing

A player that shows you the music

The Now Playing screen is the heart of the app. An animated spectrum dances above the station name, and the NOW PLAYING card shows the artist and song title live, straight from the station's stream.

Skip to the previous or next station with the side buttons, pause with the big central control, and fine-tune the loudness with the dedicated in-app volume slider. Equalizer, visualization style and sleep timer are all one tap below.

  • Live artist & song metadata from the stream
  • Previous / next station switching without leaving the player
  • Precise volume slider with percentage readout
04 · Equalizer

Shape the sound to your ears

A real audio equalizer, built in. Five frequency bands — 60 Hz, 230 Hz, 910 Hz, 3.6 kHz and 14 kHz — let you lift the bass, tame the mids or add sparkle to the highs, each adjustable from −15 to +15 dB.

Below the bands, Bass Boost adds physical punch and 3D Surround widens the stage for headphones. One switch turns the whole chain on or off, and Reset takes you back to flat in a tap.

5 bands Bass Boost 3D Surround One-tap Reset
05 · Visualizers

Five ways to watch the sound

Music you can see. Pick between Classic bars, Mirror, Radial, LED segments and Thin lines — five distinct visualizer styles that react in real time to whatever is playing.

Switching is instant: tap the Visualization button on the player, choose a style, and the spectrum redraws on the spot. Each style has its own character, from smooth classic bars to a crisp LED-segment wall.

Classic bars Mirror Radial LED segments Thin lines
06 · Sleep Timer

Fall asleep to the radio, not with it on all night

Tap 🌙 Sleep Timer and choose 15, 30, 45 or 60 minutes — the radio fades out and stops on its own once you're asleep, saving battery and data.

The same row of tools includes Share to send the station you're enjoying to a friend, a full Stop button, and the 🚗 shortcut into Car Mode.

15m 30m 45m 60m Share station
07 · Car Mode

Big buttons. Zero distraction.

On the road, small controls are dangerous. Car Mode strips the player down to what matters: a huge play/pause button you can hit without looking, the station name in oversized type, the current song, a LIVE badge and one big Stop button.

The visualizer keeps the screen alive while you drive, and Exit returns you to the full player the moment you park.

Oversized controls LIVE indicator Glance-friendly type
08 · Radio Alarm Clock

Wake up to your favorite station

Forget the default beep. World Radio's alarm clock wakes you up with live radio: set the time, pick the days of the week to repeat — Monday through Sunday — and choose exactly which station starts playing.

Set it once for workday mornings, add another for the weekend, and every day starts with music instead of noise.

Any station Repeat by weekday Multiple alarms
