Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Todor Ilinov ("TI Apps", "we", "I") handles information in the mobile applications published under this developer account.

TV DSP Center — Equalizer (com.tvdsp.center)

World Radio (com.worldradio.app)

Information we do not collect

Our apps do not require you to create an account. We do not collect your name, email address, contacts, photos, precise location, or any other personally identifying information. Audio processed by TV DSP Center never leaves your device.

Information collected automatically

The apps use the following third-party services, which may collect limited technical data:

  • Google Firebase Analytics — anonymous usage statistics (screens viewed, session length, device model, country, app version). Used to understand how the apps are used and to improve them.
  • Firebase Crashlytics — crash reports (device model, OS version, stack traces). Used to find and fix bugs.
  • Google AdMob — serves advertisements. AdMob may use an advertising identifier to show relevant ads. See Google's Advertising Policy.

This data is aggregated and does not identify you personally. It is processed by Google in accordance with the Google Privacy Policy.

Purchases

TV DSP Center offers an optional one-time in-app purchase (Pro upgrade). Payments are processed entirely by Google Play. We never see or store your payment details.

Internet radio streams

World Radio streams stations provided by publicly available directories (such as the Radio Browser project). When you play a station, your device connects directly to that broadcaster's stream server, which may log standard connection data (such as your IP address) under the broadcaster's own privacy policy.

Permissions

  • Audio settings / modify audio (TV DSP Center) — required to apply equalizer and DSP effects.
  • Internet — required for radio streaming, ads and analytics.
  • Foreground service / notifications (World Radio) — required for background playback and the alarm feature.

Children

Our apps are not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

Data deletion

Analytics data is anonymous and cannot be linked back to you. Uninstalling the app stops all data collection. You can also reset your advertising ID in your device's Google settings at any time.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, contact: tvdspcenter@gmail.com
